How to use this tool

  1. Open the relevant articles from your Google Alerts in new tabs as usual.
  2. Use an extension such as Copy All URLs to get all news items' URLs onto your clipboard, and paste them into the grey box. Hit Go!
  3. Once they're done processing, drag them around into the order you need, and edit the section titles to suit.
  4. Hit the green toggle button to format the story into a "Related Stories" section.
  5. If you need to add new dates, use the add date tool at the bottom right. They're also draggable!
  6. Once you're done, click clean up to eliminate the "toggle" switches and duplicated "Related Stories" headers.
  7. Remember to add a summary at the top, under the main heading.
  8. Finally, click copy to clipboard to select and copy the whole report. Paste that into your email, add a suject and recipients, and you're done! :D

Known bugs:

If your titles are coming through as "undefined", please use Chrome (or any browser that isn't IE, Edge, or Firefox. Should now be fixed!
